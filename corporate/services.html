Water Damage Restoration

This is our core service and the one we get the most calls for in Nationwide. Flood Cleanup

Flood damage is urgent because floodwater isn't just water — it carries sewage, chemicals, soil, and bacteria. Our Nationwide flood cleanup starts with rapid extraction using commercial pumps, followed by removal of contaminated materials (carpet pad, lower drywall, insulation). Then antimicrobial treatment, structural drying, and air quality verification. We document everything for FEMA claims when applicable.

Mold Remediation

Mold is almost guaranteed in when water damage goes untreated for more than 48 hours — the heat and humidity create perfect conditions. Our mold remediation follows IICRC S520 standards: containment with negative air pressure, removal of affected materials in sealed bags, HEPA air filtration throughout, antimicrobial treatment of remaining structures, and post-remediation air testing by a third-party lab. We don't do mold testing and mold removal — that's a conflict of interest. We recommend an independent hygienist for pre-testing.

Structural Drying

Every material dries differently — drywall, hardwood, concrete, framing lumber. Our structural drying approach accounts for that. We use a mix of axial and centrifugal air movers, LGR dehumidifiers sized for the space, and specialty systems like injectidry for wall cavities. Monitoring equipment tracks drying curves in real time. In 's climate zone, proper dehumidifier selection makes or breaks the timeline.

Nobody wants to deal with sewage — and you shouldn't have to. Our Nationwide hazmat-trained crew handles extraction, contaminated material removal, antimicrobial treatment, and disposal.
