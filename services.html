Water Damage Repair Company Services

Full-service water damage restoration for Carefree homes and businesses. Every service listed below is done by our own crews, not subcontractors.

Water Damage Restoration

Water damage restoration means getting the water out, getting the structure dry, and making sure mold doesn't take hold. Our Carefree crews use truck-mounted extraction units for speed, then transition to LGR dehumidifiers and high-velocity air movers for the drying phase. We take moisture readings every day with both pin-type and pinless meters — you'll see the numbers dropping. When readings hit dry standard, we verify with a final thermal imaging sweep. The whole process runs 3-7 days depending on how much water we're dealing with.

Flood Cleanup

Floods in Carefree tend to happen fast — monsoon rain overwhelms drainage systems, or a retention basin overflows. When that happens, you need pumps in your property immediately. Our flood cleanup crews bring submersible pumps, truck-mounted extraction units, and enough hose to reach anywhere on your property. After extraction, we strip out flood-contaminated materials (flood water is Category 3 — it's not clean), sanitize all surfaces, and set up drying equipment. We also coordinate with Carefree utilities if there's any concern about electrical safety.

Mold Remediation

Mold is almost guaranteed in Arizona when water damage goes untreated for more than 48 hours — the heat and humidity create perfect conditions. Our mold remediation follows IICRC S520 standards: containment with negative air pressure, removal of affected materials in sealed bags, HEPA air filtration throughout, antimicrobial treatment of remaining structures, and post-remediation air testing by a third-party lab. We don't do mold testing and mold removal — that's a conflict of interest. We recommend an independent hygienist for pre-testing.

Fire Damage Restoration

Our fire damage restoration covers emergency board-up, soot and smoke cleaning, odor elimination, water damage from suppression efforts, and full structural rebuild. Smoke odor is the hardest part — it gets into everything. We use a combination of thermal fogging, hydroxyl generators, and ozone treatment (in unoccupied spaces only) to eliminate it completely. In Arizona, fire restoration typically takes 2-8 weeks depending on the extent of damage.

Arizona gets its share of severe weather — dust storms, monsoon downpours, and occasional hail. Our storm damage response in Carefree starts with emergency tarping and board-up to prevent further damage. Then we assess, document, and begin mitigation. We photograph everything for insurance before we touch it, and we work with your carrier to get the claim moving fast. Roof damage, broken windows, flooding — we handle all of it.

Sewage Cleanup

Nobody wants to deal with sewage — and you shouldn't have to. Our Carefree hazmat-trained crew handles extraction, contaminated material removal, antimicrobial treatment, and disposal. We use Bio-Cide and Concrobium at professional concentrations — the stuff from Home Depot doesn't cut it for Category 3 water. Full PPE, proper disposal, and we don't leave until the area is safe.

Structural Drying

Every material dries differently — drywall, hardwood, concrete, framing lumber. Our structural drying approach accounts for that. We use a mix of axial and centrifugal air movers, LGR dehumidifiers sized for the space, and specialty systems like injectidry for wall cavities. Monitoring equipment tracks drying curves in real time. In Arizona's climate zone, proper dehumidifier selection makes or breaks the timeline.
